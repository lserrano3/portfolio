Soul Soaring Wonder

A mock exhibit for the work of Mike Perry

Objective

Expressing the essence of Mike Perry's work through a an exploration into his colorful and funky journey of the unknown.

Design Statement

Being given the ability to showcase a great artist such as Mike Perry was a blast. His work is so youthful in spirit through his chaotic patterns and zany illustrations. I believe my greatest challenge was having too much fun creating chaos and forgetting to think structurally. Despite this, I am confident I was able to capture the essence of Mike Perry's work in my design.
